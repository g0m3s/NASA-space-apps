Sea Rise

Sea levels are rising around the world, putting people living in islands and coastal zones in danger and bringing several possible consequences, including even damages to human health.

The Challenge

The Rising Water challengeâ€™s goal is to help communicate the impacts of rising oceans by creating a visualization tool that illustrates the changes caused by this problem.

Our proposal

We seeked to create an engaging application for people to learn about the causes, consequences and possible solutions to the issue of rising sea levels. To do so, we made use of visual tools such as interactive graphs and gamification concepts. The result is SeeRise.

Quizz

This function presents the user a quizz about their habits and actions and how they relate to sea level rise. According to the result of the quizz, several suggestions are made to the user on how they can further help minimize the rate at which the oceans are rising (spoiler: demanding action from governments is a huge part of the solution).

Causes and Consequences

In this section the user can learn about what causes sea levels to rise and the consequences of it, along with pictures to illustrate the phenomenons, provided by NASA's Images of Change and Climate 365. We also utilized data on the causes of sea level rise made available by NASA's Infographic on Sea Level Rise.

Projection

The app uses the location of the user to calculate if the area they're in is a potential risk zone and how long would it take for water to take over the place according to the current rate of sea level rise, made available by NASA's Sea Level Change Portal.

Solutions

This sections informs the user about the possible solutions to mitigate or to adapt to sea level rise and the several impacts presented in the Causes and Consequences function, from NASA's Global Climate Change Portal.
